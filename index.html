About us

General.

MySight Shopping is a clothing and fashion design brand Established in the year 2021, in South Africa, Pretoria, by two parteners.  The name MySight comes from a thaught and vision that the brand brings amongst its customers.  The brand empowers vision and insight, hence the name MySight, which comes from the creative thaught of insight into one's future,  and the slogan;  "Its yours, own it." which encourages those who wear and live by the brand to look deep into their objectives and goals for the future ensuring that they are setting foundations and solid building blocks to ensure sustainability in what they believe in and have planned for their future.


Where our name comes from.

The Name MySight and the slogan "Its yours, Own it." come together to reveal the brand's vision,  goals and focus to support the growth of its customers in satisfying their needs and creating opportunities with the programmes planned for the future,   to meet changes in the customer community needs whilst building strong relationships.  At MySight,  we stand together with out people.

Area of operation.

We are currently based in Pretoria, South Africa and in Margate, Kwazulu-Natal, South Africa. we offer ontime delivery throughout the whole of South africa and work to ensure that our customers receive their orders on time and are happy with their products.
